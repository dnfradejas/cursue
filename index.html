Who We Are

In February 1, 2000 Engr. Mary Stephanie Carandang, and four of his Co – Engineers founded the Construe Construction Service Corporation to handle masonry construction and other services related to construction. From the beginning, The Construe Construction Family infused its company with strong core values.

History

It is founded in 2000; CCS Corporation’s main office is in Romblon, IV-B MIMAROPA Phil. That actively continues with a project of network services in Canada, Thailand, USA and South Korea with numerous project offices.

Partnership

Partnering with CCS Corporation provides your construction project with high standard materials and services for affordable and honest price with a experience for success. Our team hails for expertise in the field of construction and combine innovative construction methods and accountable project management to get the job done and get it done right.

Mission

Our mission is to provide the best possible service to our customers and we have strict quality, and safety methods to make sure you are getting the best choice for construction.

Vission

Our vision is to be an industry expert in the construction industry and the leading provider of delivering materials and services with safety.
